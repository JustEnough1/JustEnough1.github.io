> My journey_

2019

Embarking on the Journey

Exploring the World of Programming

In 2019 I started showing genuine interest in programming and delved into the exciting world of coding as a hobby.

Self-Learning for Fun

I embarked on a self-learning journey to understand the fundamentals of programming languages out of sheer curiosity and enthusiasm.

Choosing the Path

After my graduation from school, I made a decisive choice to pursue a career in software development by enrolling in a vocational school to formalize and deepen programming skills.

2020 - 2021

Expanding Horizons

Learning the Languages

I embraced the world of programming languages, acquiring proficiency in Python and Java. Also gained expertise in web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.

Growing Confidence

By the end of 2021, felt a remarkable boost in confidence in coding abilities and a sense of fulfillment, confirming that the decision to pursue a career as a software developer was on the right track.

2022

Broadening Skills and Gaining Experience

Mastering Web Technologies

I deepened expertise in web technologies, including JavaScript for dynamic and interactive web applications, Socket.IO for real-time communication, and Selenium for automated testing.

Advancing Skills and Real-World Impact
